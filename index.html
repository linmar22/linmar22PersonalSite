Why you should hire me

Well, for starters, I built this website, which is responsive, in one day. However, do not let me mislead you - speed is not what I strive for; not unless it is demanded. In my book quality > speed. "The Zen of Python" and the "Manifesto for Agile Software Development" are top priority guidelines when developing anything from a large scale software suite to a small website like this.

Throughout the years I lived in countries such as Germany (3 years), the United States (1 year), and Denmark (2 years and counting). These multicultural surroundings have developed my social abilities to interact to the fullest. Team driven development is not something I have a problem with.

In terms of writing code, my standards are high. I leave plenty of comments and structure everything pedantically. Over my studies and work experience, i've become familiar with a wide variety of technologies and concepts. I believe the table below will summarize it quite well.
